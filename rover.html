Conceptual Subterranean Rover Design

I led a team to retrofit an unmanned rover for a simulated mine rescue mission, focusing on optimizing its mobility and functionality. Using SolidWorks for 3D modeling, ANSYS for finite element analysis (FEA), and detailed hand calculations, we developed a robust design capable of navigating challenging terrain. My responsibilities included overseeing concept development, as well as conducting most design and analysis. The result was a mission ready rover design which satisfied client requirements identified through problem scoping. This project showcases my ability to lead technical teams and deliver innovative solutions under complex constraints.
