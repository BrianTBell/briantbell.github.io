R&D · Robotics

Cobot-Enabled Workcell Platform

Technical Lead for a commercial high-precision dual-cobot manufacturing platform supporting tele-operated and autonomous operation. Owned the system end to end: architecture, electromechanical integration, workcell layout, Universal Robots integration, custom CAD, electrical setup, and component selection.

Designed and built the PC-based control system from the ground up: real-time control logic, state-machine sequencing, an operator HMI for both operating modes, sensor fusion, and OpenCV ArUco visual servoing, backed by an NVIDIA Isaac Sim digital twin. Coordinated verification across accuracy, precision, cycle time, and functional performance, briefed C-level leadership and clients directly, and contributed to R&D proposals for continued development. The result was a working prototype platform that reduced manual effort and proved the automation approach.
